MR MORALE AND THE BIG STEPPERS OUT NOW

More than five years since his last solo full-length LP, Kendrick Lamar (or now known as Oklama) is back with a double-album in Mr Morale and the Big Steppers.

Channeling his thoughts, Oklama painstakingly tackles his shortcomings, his relationships with money, hypocrisy on white women, his relationship with his father, the limits of his loyalty, love in the context of heteronormative relationships, motivation, embedded responsibility, gender, and generational trauma. Dense, thought-provoking, and multilayered across innovative beats.
It's a difficult listen, but ultimately human.
